What's new this November?



Things are getting lit in Birmingham City Centre as Christmas approaches. To ensure your festive season is merry and bright we've listed all our personal favourites and recommendations on how to enjoy the season below.

The arrival of Birmingham's Christmas market marks the beginning of the magical season. Being the largest authentic market outside of Germany or Austria, this Frankfurt market offers a large range of traditional gifts and a selection of tempting food and drink. Located in the same place as last year, Victoria Square and New Street, the market is a great social event with lots to be enjoyed including live music.

It's back! Enjoy some of the city's most impressive sights from Birmingham's Big Wheel, located on Centenary Square, open now until the 12th of January. Alongside this, the new and improved ice rink which is now weatherproof, ensuring you can enjoy yourself without having to worry about the weather forecast. And good news, sudents receive a discounted price! Click here for more details.

Journey into the centre of Birmingham where Bompas & Parr premier their festive tea parlour and party bar from the 14th of November. The Christmas pop-up, which is based outside the Bullring & Grand Central, features 88,000 fairy lights over 9km of the city - that's more festive lights per square foot than anywhere else in the country! Fancy it? Follow the link to explore their festive tea menu and selection of illuminating cocktails. Whilst entry is free, we wouldn't want you to miss out, so you can also buy tickets here for guranteed entry.
